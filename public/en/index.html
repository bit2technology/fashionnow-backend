See how it works:

Doubt in what to wear?

What clothes to go to that party, which nail polish to use at work, which make up to go to a marriage or what shoes to wear just for hanging out?

Put an end on your doubts!

Take a picture with each outfit and create a poll in Fashion Now.

Ask your friends!

No one better than friends to help you decide! Select those who will help you or send a public poll so all users of Fashion Now will help you.

Have fun!

After receiving the votes of your poll, you will know which of the two outifts your friends preferred and will be certain that made the best decision!
